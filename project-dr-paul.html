About

Dr. Paul is a UK-based pet wellness brand offering a full range of natural, vet-formulated supplements for dogs — from calming chews and joint support to probiotic blends and salmon oil.

The brief: build an e-commerce experience that communicates trust, warmth, and clinical credibility — while driving conversions from a health-conscious pet owner audience.

Skills used:

Web Design UI / UX E-Commerce Copywriting Brand Design Figma

Building Trust for
Every Dog Owner

Pet wellness is a market built on one thing: trust. Dog owners making decisions about their pet's health need to feel completely confident in the brand before they buy. The Dr. Paul website was designed from the ground up to answer that need — leading with clinical credibility (GMP certified, ISO certified, vet-formulated) before ever showing a product price.

The site needed to work across two very different audiences: first-time buyers discovering natural supplements for their dog, and repeat customers looking for a fast, frictionless purchase. The information architecture was designed to serve both — with a clear product range, benefit-led copywriting, and a smooth checkout experience that removes hesitation at every step.

The warm, natural palette echoes the brand's "100% natural" promise — earthy tones paired with clean white space to keep the focus on product photography and trust signals. Every page is structured to guide the visitor from curiosity to confidence to conversion.
