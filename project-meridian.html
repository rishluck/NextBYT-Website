The Meridian

Client

Scope
Architecture & 3D Visualisation

♦ The Brief

Mixed-Use
Development

Meridian Properties approached us with a bold vision — a landmark mixed-use tower for Dubai's rapidly evolving business district. The client needed photorealistic 3D visualisations and detailed architectural concepts to present to investors and planning authorities before any physical work began.

The brief called for a design that balanced commercial viability with architectural ambition — a building that would stand out on the skyline while meeting strict local planning codes and sustainability requirements.

We developed the concept from initial sketches through to fully rendered walkthroughs, giving stakeholders a clear, compelling picture of the finished development.

♦ Project Scope

  • Concept development & spatial planning
  • AutoCAD floor plans (all levels)
  • Exterior 3D modelling & rendering
  • Interior common-area visualisations
  • Photorealistic daylight & night renders
  • Animated 360° walkthrough video
  • Investor presentation deck
  • Planning submission documentation

♦ Our Approach

How We
Delivered It

01
Site & Context Analysis

We studied the site location, surrounding buildings, solar orientation, and Dubai's planning codes to establish a design framework rooted in context rather than trend.

02
Concept & Massing

Multiple massing options were explored in early 3D modelling to test height, floor plate efficiency, and visual impact before committing to the final form.

03
Photorealistic Rendering

Final renders were produced with cinematic lighting, accurate material textures, and surrounding context to give investors a genuine sense of the finished development.
