♦ Project Overview

A Shopify Store Built
to Convert

Vitly Nutrition is a UK-based health and wellness brand selling premium vitamin gummy supplements — from Apple Cider Vinegar and Omega 3-6-9 to Hair, Skin & Nails and Pre+Pro Biotic blends. They are one of the UK and Europe's first B Corp Certified gummy nutrition brands.

The brief was to design and build a full Shopify storefront that matched the brand's vibrant, health-forward identity — using bold lifestyle photography, a warm teal and cream colour palette, and a clean layout that guides shoppers from discovery through to checkout with minimal friction.

Every section was designed with conversion in mind: a high-impact hero, a scrolling ingredient marquee, editorial split-layout philosophy sections, a bestsellers product grid with sale badges, a features trust bar, and a campaign lifestyle section — all built on Shopify for easy client management.

♦ What Was Built

Key Store Features

Every element of the store was built to reflect Vitly's brand positioning — playful, health-conscious, science-backed, and trustworthy.

🦸
Hero Banner
Full-width editorial hero with lifestyle model photography, brand tagline, and a prominent "Shop Now" CTA designed to hook visitors immediately.
🏷️
Scrolling Marquee
Animated ingredient ticker — Vinegar Powder, Blackcurrant, Vitamin B, Iron, Inositol, Glucose Syrup — reinforcing product quality and naturalness.
🛒
Bestsellers Grid
Product collection grid with sale badges, star ratings, and price comparisons — built to surface the best-selling SKUs and drive add-to-cart.
✅
Trust Bar
Four-point trust section: Delicious Vitamin Gummies, Science-Backed Nutrition, Expertly Formulated, and B Corp Certified — addressing key buyer objections.
📸
Campaign Section
Bold three-panel lifestyle campaign imagery with a centred "Shop Now" CTA — creating an emotional connection with the brand's fun, vibrant identity.
📧
Newsletter Signup
"Join the club" footer email capture for product announcements and exclusive discounts — growing the brand's owned marketing channel.
🌍
Multi-Region Ready
Built with UK market as primary, with language and region selectors in the footer for future international expansion.
📱
Mobile Optimised
Every section designed and tested to be fully responsive — ensuring the shopping experience is seamless on smartphones, where most orders come from.
