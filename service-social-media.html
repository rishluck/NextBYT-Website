♦ FAQs

Common Questions

Answers to what most clients ask before signing up.

How many posts per week do you publish? +
It depends on your package. Our Growth plan includes daily posting on your primary platform and 4–5 posts per week on secondary platforms. We always prioritise quality over quantity.
Do I need to approve content before it goes live? +
Yes — we prepare a full month of content in advance and share it for your review via a shared content calendar. You approve, request changes, or leave it to us. Nothing goes live without sign-off unless you prefer a fully managed approach.
Will you manage my paid ad budget too? +
Yes. We manage paid social campaigns as part of our Growth and Enterprise packages. Ad spend budget is separate from our management fee. We'll recommend a realistic budget based on your goals during onboarding.
How quickly will I see results? +
Organic growth takes time — most clients see meaningful results within 60–90 days. Paid ads can show results within days. We set realistic expectations upfront and track progress monthly.
