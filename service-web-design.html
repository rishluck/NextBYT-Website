♦ FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project with us.

How long does a website project take? +
A standard 5–10 page business website typically takes 3–5 weeks from kick-off to launch. E-commerce sites and custom web apps can take 6–10 weeks depending on complexity. We provide a detailed timeline at project start.
Do you provide hosting and domain registration? +
Yes — we can set up and manage hosting for you, or deploy to your existing host. We work with all major providers. We recommend managed WordPress hosting or Vercel for most projects.
Will I be able to update the website myself? +
Absolutely. We build with easy-to-use CMS backends and provide full training so your team can update content, add blog posts, and make changes without needing a developer.
What is included in post-launch support? +
All projects include 30 days of free post-launch support covering bug fixes and minor content updates. We also offer monthly maintenance retainers for ongoing support, updates, and performance monitoring.
Do you work with clients outside the UK? +
Yes — we work with clients globally. Our process is fully remote-friendly with video calls, shared design tools, and clear communication throughout. We have successfully delivered projects across Europe, the Middle East, and North America.
