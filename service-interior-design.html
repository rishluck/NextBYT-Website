♦ FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your interior design project.

Do you only design for new builds or can you work with existing properties? +
We work with both new builds and existing properties. For existing spaces, we can work from accurate photographs, measurements, and floor plans — no site visit required for the design phase, though we recommend one for larger projects.
Will I receive drawings my builder can use? +
Yes — our Interior Design Package includes a full specification schedule with all materials, dimensions, and supplier details. We can also produce detailed technical drawings for joinery, tiling, and fitted furniture if required.
Can you help me source and purchase the furniture and materials? +
Yes — as an optional add-on, we offer procurement support where we source, order, and manage delivery of furniture and materials on your behalf. This removes significant stress from the fit-out process.
How many rooms can you design at once? +
There's no limit. We regularly handle full property designs across multiple floors and dozens of rooms simultaneously. Our project management process ensures everything is coordinated and delivered on time.
