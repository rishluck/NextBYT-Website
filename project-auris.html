♦ Project Overview

A Fashion Store Built
for Discernment

AURIS is a luxury fashion brand with a philosophy rooted in craft, permanence, and quiet confidence. The brand's ethos — "We do not make clothes for the moment. We make clothes that become a part of who you are." — demanded a digital presence that matched that same level of intention.

The brief was to build an editorial Shopify storefront that could carry seasonal collections — starting with The Art of Silence Collection and The Autumn Edit — while feeling timeless rather than trend-led. Every section was designed to slow the visitor down, invite consideration, and position AURIS as a brand worth knowing.

The result is a refined, image-led e-commerce experience with strong editorial layout, luxurious whitespace, and a warm neutral palette that echoes the brand's tactile, cloth-and-craft identity.

♦ What Was Built

Key Site Features

Each section of the AURIS site was designed to serve a distinct purpose in the luxury customer journey — from discovery to desire to purchase.

🖼️
Editorial Hero
A full-bleed collection hero with fashion photography, a bold serif headline, and a restrained CTA — setting the tone immediately as a luxury destination.
🍂
Seasonal Collections
The Autumn Edit — a curated product grid with editorial-style imagery, product naming, and pricing laid out to feel like a magazine spread, not a catalogue.
🧥
Feature Product Page
Le Manteau Doré — a dedicated product feature with full-width photography, hand-written-style typography, and clear collection/size/colour selectors.
📸
Season Stories
A mosaic-style campaign gallery featuring editorial lifestyle photography — creating an emotional visual narrative around the season's aesthetic.
🪡
Brand Story
Craft That Endures — a brand history section anchored to 2014, reinforcing longevity and the brand's commitment to quality over trends.
⭐
Social Proof
A customer quote section with editorial typography — "Wearing AURIS is not about being seen." — designed to resonate emotionally rather than sell rationally.
✉️
Letters from the Atelier
A newsletter section framed as correspondence rather than marketing — building an intimate subscriber relationship that feels aligned with the brand voice.
📱
Mobile Optimised
Every section designed to be fully responsive — the editorial feel carries seamlessly from desktop to mobile without losing the luxury aesthetic.
