♦ The Brief

Luxury Residence

A private client commissioned us to redesign their four-bedroom London home from the ground up. The brief was clear — warm, contemporary luxury that felt personal rather than showroom-perfect. Natural materials, layered lighting, and spaces that genuinely worked for family life.

We were responsible for the full interior concept — from spatial planning and material selection through to photorealistic 3D visualisations that guided the build team throughout the renovation.

Every room was designed with both aesthetics and function in mind, creating a home that works beautifully on an ordinary Tuesday as much as it does for entertaining.

♦ Project Scope

  • Full spatial planning (4 bed, 3 bath)
  • Mood boards & concept direction
  • Material & finish specifications
  • Bespoke furniture layout plans
  • 3D interior visualisations (all rooms)
  • Lighting design & schemes
  • Contractor coordination drawings
  • Site visits & sign-off support

♦ Our Approach

How We
Delivered It

01
Client Discovery

A detailed lifestyle consultation helped us understand how the family actually lives — which rooms they use most, their daily routines, storage needs, and the aesthetic they aspired to.

02
Concept & Material Boards

Three distinct concept directions were presented with full mood boards, material samples, and colour stories before the client selected and refined their preferred direction.

03
3D Visualisation

Photorealistic renders of every key space let the client experience their new home before a single wall was painted — reducing change orders and ensuring alignment with contractors.
