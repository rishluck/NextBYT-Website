♦ FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before commissioning architectural visualisation.

What files do you need to start a render project? +
We typically need architectural drawings (CAD, PDF, or even hand sketches), your material and finish specifications, and any reference images. The more detail you provide, the more accurate the render. We can work from early-stage concepts too.
How photorealistic are the renders? +
Our renders are frequently mistaken for photographs by end clients and investors. We use physically accurate lighting, high-resolution materials, and detailed environmental context to achieve maximum realism.
Can you work with buildings that haven't been designed yet? +
Yes — we work at all project stages from initial concept to detailed design. Early-stage renders are a great way to test massing and design options quickly before committing to full detailed design.
How long does it take to produce architectural renders? +
A standard set of 3–5 exterior renders typically takes 5–10 working days. Animated walkthroughs take 2–4 weeks depending on length and complexity. We always confirm timelines at brief stage.
