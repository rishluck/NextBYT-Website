About

A concept website design for Malabar Hill — a private jungle retreat in Sri Lanka offering 14 exclusive villas with ocean views, plunge pools, and immersive nature experiences.

The brief was to capture the feeling of complete seclusion and luxury while making villa booking and discovery effortless for high-net-worth travellers.

Skills used:

UI Design UX Web Design Figma Hospitality

Designing for the
Art of Escape

Malabar Hill is a concept for an ultra-luxury boutique resort perched above the ocean and cradled by the jungle of Sri Lanka. The design challenge was to translate a feeling — silence, seclusion, timeless design — into a digital experience that compels its audience to book.

The website leads with a full-bleed hero that puts the architecture centre stage, showcasing the resort's signature arched pool villas draped in tropical greenery. Every scroll reveals something new: the reservation widget for frictionless booking, a cinematic story section, villa type comparisons, immersive dining features, and a weddings & events showcase.

A warm terracotta and ivory palette — drawn directly from the resort's earthy architecture — creates visual continuity between the physical space and its digital presence. Refined serif typography communicates exclusivity, while generous white space gives the imagery room to breathe.
