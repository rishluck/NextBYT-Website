Redesigning a Global
Beauty Giant

This is a concept redesign of Sephora's homepage — one of the world's most recognised beauty e-commerce brands. The goal was to modernise the layout, improve visual hierarchy, and create a more immersive shopping experience that converts browsers into buyers.

The redesign focuses on bold hero sections that highlight promotions like "Celebrate Pay Day" with clear CTAs, improved product grid layouts for featured collections such as "Father's Day Fragrances" and "Beauty Picks Just For You", and a cleaner overall structure that guides the customer naturally from discovery to purchase.

This project demonstrates the ability to work at scale — understanding how a global brand communicates, then improving it with stronger design decisions while staying true to the brand's identity.

🎯
Clear Visual Hierarchy
Redesigned the homepage layout to guide the user eye from hero promotion down to featured products and category discovery — reducing cognitive load and increasing scroll depth.
🛍️
Conversion-First Design
Every section — from the hero banner to the product grid — was designed with conversion in mind. Strong CTAs, trust signals, and a clean checkout flow prompt action at every step.
📱
Mobile-First Thinking
The layout was conceived mobile-first, ensuring the experience is equally strong on smartphones — where the majority of beauty shoppers browse and buy.
