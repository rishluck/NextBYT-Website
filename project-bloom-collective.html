♦ The Brief

Full Rebrand

Bloom Collective — a growing wellness and lifestyle brand — came to us with a brand that had outgrown itself. Their original identity was built in a weekend when they first launched. Three years and significant growth later, it no longer reflected where they were heading.

The brief was to create a premium, cohesive brand identity that could carry the business into its next chapter — scaling from a boutique local community to a nationally recognised wellness brand.

We delivered a complete brand system: logo, colour palette, typography hierarchy, photography guidelines, brand voice, and a full digital and print asset suite — everything they needed to show up consistently and confidently.

♦ Project Scope

  • Brand strategy & positioning
  • Logo design (primary + variations)
  • Colour palette & typography system
  • Brand guidelines document
  • Social media templates (12 formats)
  • Business stationery suite
  • Email signature design
  • Packaging design concepts

♦ Our Approach

How We
Delivered It

01
Brand Discovery

A brand workshop with the founders helped us uncover the values, personality, and positioning that would make Bloom genuinely distinct — not just visually different, but strategically grounded.

02
Identity Design

Three distinct brand directions were developed and presented in full — each with rationale. The chosen direction was then refined through collaborative feedback rounds until it was exactly right.

03
System Build-Out

The approved identity was systematised into a comprehensive brand toolkit — every asset, template, and guideline needed to roll out the new brand confidently across every touchpoint.
