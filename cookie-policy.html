1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

This policy explains what cookies we use on the NextByt website, why we use them, and how you can control them.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Understand how visitors interact with our site (analytics)
  • Remember your preferences across pages
  • Improve performance and page load speed

We do not use cookies for advertising or to track you across other websites.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled. Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g. Google Analytics). All data is anonymised. Up to 2 years
Preference Remember settings such as language or display preferences. Up to 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services we use, including:

  • Google Analytics — used to analyse website traffic and usage patterns. Data is anonymised and not personally identifiable.
  • Google Fonts — used to serve web fonts. May log your IP address.
  • Cloudflare — used for security and performance. Sets security cookies to protect the site from malicious traffic.

These third parties have their own cookie and privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them directly.

5. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings — most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies via their settings menu
  • Opt-out tools — you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Device settings — mobile devices have options to limit ad tracking in their privacy settings

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legislation. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at hello@nextbyt.com.
