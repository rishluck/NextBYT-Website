♦ FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our branding process.

How long does a brand identity project take? +
A full brand identity project typically takes 3–4 weeks from brief to final delivery. Logo-only projects can be completed in 1–2 weeks. Timelines depend on feedback speed and revision rounds.
Do I own the logo once the project is complete? +
Yes — upon final payment, full intellectual property rights transfer to you. You own the logo outright. We provide written IP transfer documentation as standard.
What if I don't like any of the initial concepts? +
We always present 3+ concepts and our clients consistently find at least one strong direction. If none resonate, we go back to the drawing board. We work until you're happy — that's our commitment.
Can you redesign my existing logo without a full rebrand? +
Absolutely. We offer logo refresh projects that modernise your existing mark while preserving brand recognition. This is quicker and more cost-effective than starting from scratch.
