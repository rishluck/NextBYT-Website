♦ Project Overview

A Restaurant Brand Built
with Attitude

Ashly Fishbar is a bold, no-nonsense food brand with a simple promise — Honest. Crispy. Always Hot. With five counter locations and one central kitchen, the brand needed a digital presence that matched its character: direct, confident, and full of personality.

The brief was to design a website and brand system that could communicate value (two prices, one promise), build local trust ("What the locals say"), and convert hungry visitors into customers with a clear, friction-free experience — from menu to "Ring Us."

The result is a high-energy, bold design system rooted in red, black, and yellow — a classic fast-food colour palette used with intention — combined with sharp, direct copywriting that gives Ashly Fishbar a voice as distinctive as the food.

♦ What Was Built

Key Site Features

Every section of the Ashly Fishbar site was designed with one goal — turn a hungry visitor into a paying customer as fast as possible.

🔥
Bold Hero
"Honest. Crispy. Always Hot." — a punchy, full-bleed hero with food photography and the brand's three-word promise front and centre.
💷
Transparent Pricing
Two prices, one promise. A clean pricing section that communicates value upfront — building trust with no-nonsense clarity before the customer even sees the menu.
🗺️
Five Locations
A clear five-counter location section — helping local customers instantly identify their nearest Ashly Fishbar and reducing the barrier to visit.
🍔
Food Showcase
High-appetite food photography section showing the full range — from crispy fish to loaded burgers — with bold headlines designed to trigger cravings.
⭐
Social Proof
"What the locals say" — a review section using real customer voices to build community trust and local credibility, presented in an energetic, readable layout.
🌿
Brand Values
"100% Natural. We don't foul it." — a dedicated section communicating ingredient quality and sourcing transparency in the brand's own voice.
📞
Direct CTA
"Hungry? Ring Us." — a bold, personality-led closing CTA that drives action through voice rather than a generic "Contact Us" button.
📱
Mobile First
Fully responsive design built for mobile — where most food decisions are made — with fast-loading images and a frictionless tap-to-call experience.
